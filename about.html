Oompah Brass are an award-winning 5-piece brass ensemble renowned for their energetic shows and incredible musicality.

Described by Chris Evans as the ‘Legends in Lederhosen’, Oompah Brass came together in 2006 and have since performed extensively around the UK and the world.

Their broad repertoire includes the traditional Bavarian classics, however, you are more likely to hear them performing in a style which they have branded “Oom-pop”; modern popular music with a Bavarian twist. Artists they cover vary from Queen, the Beatles, Led Zeppelin and Guns 'n' Roses through to Oasis, Dua Lipa, Beyonce and Backstreet Boys. You can listen to their music on the Media page.

For over ten years, Oompah Brass have held residencies at two of London’s most popular bierkellers; Katzenjammers and the Octoberfest Pub. They have performed at major UK festivals including Bestival and the Isle of Wight Festival, as well as headlining Oktoberfest shows around the world. Want to know when you can see Oompah Brass in action? Check out the Gigs page for a list of public events.

Winners of the 2012 Haizetara International Street Band Competition, they are also busy performing at weddings, corporate events and award ceremonies.
